# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to a professional, respectful open-source community. Everyone
participating in this project should be able to contribute without harassment,
abuse, or exclusion.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a healthy community include:

- using clear and respectful language;
- focusing criticism on the work, design, or tradeoff;
- accepting constructive feedback;
- assuming good faith while still asking for evidence;
- helping contributors understand security and runner-safety expectations.

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- harassment, insults, or personal attacks;
- publishing another person's private information without permission;
- sexualized language or imagery;
- deliberately disruptive conduct;
- encouraging unsafe handling of credentials, runner infrastructure, or secrets.

## Enforcement

Report unacceptable behavior privately to the maintainers. Maintainers will
review reports promptly and fairly, protect reporter privacy, and take action
proportionate to the issue.
